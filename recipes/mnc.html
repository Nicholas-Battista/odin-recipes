Simple Macaroni and Cheese

Description

Mac and cheese is a classic comfort food celebrated for its creamy and indulgent flavors. This dish features tender elbow macaroni enveloped in a velvety cheese sauce that's rich, savory, and oh-so-satisfying. It's a delightful blend of pasta and cheesy goodness that warms the heart with each creamy bite.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni or pasta of your choice
  • Cheese (commonly cheddar or a blend of cheeses)
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Salt and pepper (for seasoning)
  • Optional toppings (such as breadcrumbs or grated cheese for a crispy topping)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta:
    • Boil the elbow macaroni or pasta according to package instructions until it's al dente.
    • Drain the cooked pasta and set it aside.
  2. Make the Cheese Sauce:
    • In a saucepan over medium heat, melt a few tablespoons of butter.
    • If desired, stir in a couple of tablespoons of flour to create a roux. Cook for a minute until it turns a light golden color.
    • Gradually whisk in milk to create a smooth sauce, and let it simmer for a few minutes until it thickens.
    • Add shredded cheese (e.g., cheddar) to the sauce and stir until it melts and becomes creamy.
    • Season the cheese sauce with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Combine Pasta and Cheese Sauce:
    • Mix the cooked pasta and the cheese sauce together until the pasta is well-coated.
  4. Add Toppings (Optional):
    • For an extra touch, sprinkle breadcrumbs or grated cheese on top of the mac and cheese.
  5. Serve:
    • Serve your homemade mac and cheese hot, and enjoy its creamy, cheesy goodness!
