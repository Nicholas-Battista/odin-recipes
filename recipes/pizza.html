Elote Pizza

Description

Elote pizza is a flavor-packed delight that brings the essence of Mexican street corn to a pizza crust. It features a creamy and tangy base of mayonnaise and sour cream, topped with grilled corn kernels, crumbled cotija cheese, and a sprinkle of chili powder for a hint of spice. This fusion of savory and slightly sweet flavors creates a uniquely satisfying pizza experience that's both creamy and zesty with a touch of smokiness from the grilled corn.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or pre-made pizza crust
  • Mayonnaise
  • Sour cream
  • Grilled or roasted corn kernels (fresh or canned)
  • Crumbled cotija cheese
  • Chili powder or Taj√≠n seasoning (for a hint of spice)
  • Fresh cilantro (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough.
  3. Spread the mayo-sour cream sauce.
  4. Add corn and cotija cheese.
  5. Sprinkle with chili powder.
  6. Bake until golden and bubbly.
  7. Optional: Garnish with cilantro.
  8. Slice and savor the creamy, tangy, and slightly spicy elote pizza. Enjoy!
